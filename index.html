01 — The Heritage

The Loom
& The Legacy

Jharkhand is the soul of India's "Ahimsa" Tussar Silk. JHARCRAFT's mission to empower rural artisans and weavers has preserved centuries-old traditions of organic silk farming, Kantha stitching, and tribal hand-painting.

It is time to transition these exquisite textiles from regional handicrafts to high-fashion global womenswear. We propose a platform that bridges traditional weavers with elite fashion consumers.

02 — The Concept

A 1-Day Immersive
Womenswear Showcase

An exclusive, highly curated 1-day fashion event in Ranchi, designed to spotlight JHARCRAFT's handloom textiles through the lens of modern womenswear. Attended by national press, buyers, and high-net-worth individuals.

Artisan Meets Designer
Collaborative collections where top national designers use JHARCRAFT's Tussar silk to create contemporary womenswear silhouettes.
Live Craft Exhibitions
Interactive zones demonstrating the journey of Tussar silk, Dhokra casting, and Paitkar painting during the event.
Global B2B Networking
Facilitated buying sessions connecting JHARCRAFT clusters directly with luxury retail buyers and boutique owners.

04 — The Stage

Iconic Ranchi
Venues

Moving away from sterile convention halls, we propose hosting the runway against the dramatic backdrop of Ranchi's heritage and natural landmarks.

Jagannath Temple Complex
A 17th-century hilltop temple offering a majestic, spiritual backdrop for a heritage-focused runway.
Tagore Hill
A cultural landmark associated with Rabindranath Tagore, providing panoramic sunset views for an elite evening show.
Hundru Falls Ecosystem
An avant-garde natural setting, aligning perfectly with the organic, Ahimsa nature of JHARCRAFT's silk.
